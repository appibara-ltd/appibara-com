## 3. What personal data do we collect?

Depending on your interaction with us, we may collect the following categories of data:

- **Contact data:** name, email address, and message content when you contact us  
- **Account data:** basic account information required to provide our services

- **Usage Data (Registered Mode):** For registered users, we store generation history and associated assets to provide access across sessions.

We only collect personal data that is **necessary, relevant, and limited** to its intended purpose.

We process personal data for the following purposes:

- To communicate with you and respond to inquiries or support requests  
- To improve and develop our products  
- To comply with legal and regulatory obligations  

Personal data is processed based on **legitimate interests**, **contractual necessity**, or **legal obligations**, where applicable.
