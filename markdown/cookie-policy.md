# Cookies Policy

**Last updated:** 19 January 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how **Appibara Ltd** (“Company”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) uses cookies and similar technologies on our website.

For more information about how we process personal data, please see our **Privacy Policy**.

---

## 1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device. We may also use "Local Storage" or "Session Storage" in our mobile applications and websites to store certain technical data required for the service to function properly. These technologies help us improve your user experience and ensure the security of our services.

---

## 2. How do we use cookies?

We use cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

- To ensure the basic functionality and security of our services.
- To store user preferences (e.g., selected AI model or UI settings).
- To understand how visitors interact with our website using analytics.  

We do **not** use cookies for advertising or marketing purposes.

---

## 3. Technologies we use

### Strictly necessary cookies

We use local storage within our applications to maintain your session and preferences. These are essential for:

- Maintaining your "Guest" or "User" session status.
- Temporary storage of AI-generated content during a session.

---

### Analytics cookies

Google Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies to analyze how visitors use the Website. Information generated by your use (such as a truncated IP address and device information) is processed by Google on our behalf to evaluate website activity.

For more information about how Google Analytics processes data, you can visit:
- https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/7318509
- https://policies.google.com/privacy


---

## 4. Legal Basis

We use analytics technologies only where permitted under applicable data protection laws. The processing of data by third parties like Google is subject to their own privacy policies.


---

## 5. Managing Your Preferences

You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings. For our mobile applications, you can manage data through the application settings or by clearing the application cache on your device.

Please note that disabling these technologies may affect the functionality of our services.
