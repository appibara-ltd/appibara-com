# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 15 February 2026

SelfyMon ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect
- **Anonymous Identifiers:** We use Supabase Anonymous Auth. We do not collect your name, email, or phone number. We identify you through a unique device-generated ID.
- **User Content (Photos):** To provide our AI services, you upload a selfie. These photos are stored temporarily on our secure Supabase servers.
- **Device Information:** We may collect basic device info (model, OS version) via RevenueCat and OneSignal to ensure technical stability.

## 2. How We Process Your Data
- **AI Processing:** Your uploaded photos are processed using Google Gemini AI API. This processing is strictly for generating the requested output.
- **Storage and Deletion:** - Your original and generated photos are stored for a maximum of **30 days**.
    - Once you download and confirm the result within the app, we initiate a manual or automatic deletion process from our servers.
- **No Biometric Training:** We **do not** use your photos to train our AI models or for any facial recognition purposes.

## 3. Third-Party Services
We share necessary data with the following providers to operate our service:
- **Supabase:** Secure database and cloud storage.
- **Google AI (Gemini):** Image analysis and generation.
- **RevenueCat:** Management of in-app purchases and subscriptions.
- **OneSignal:** Push notifications regarding your processing status.

## 4. Data Retention
We aim for data minimization. Your images are automatically purged after 30 days of inactivity or upon your request within the app.

## 5. Children's Privacy
SelfyMon is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
